Welcome to Kavi Art Studio!

Hi! This is Kavitha. Congratulations! You have reached a versatile artist who is passionate about fine arts. I can help you learn the art that you are looking for using a very personalized approach.

I have more than 13 years of teaching experience on various types of art and across age groups. I started my career at Viman Nagar, Pune in 2004. I moved to Hyderabad in 2010, conducted classes in and around Jubilee Hills and presently I have my institute at OU Colony, Manikonda, just 1km off old Mumbai road.

Classes are held in a serene atmosphere in a pent house with A/C and open terrace area.

In specific cases, I am offering online classes also.

I also conduct Fashion Design (Embroidery & Tailoring) classes exclusively for Ladies.
